<p>
  Website to generate personalized meal plans based on the user's food preferences and schedule.
</p>

<p>
  Enter the number of calories and number of meals you want to eat (or let Meal Maker calculate it for you!).
  <br />
  You also have the option to alter the breakdown of the macrosnutrients.
</p>
<img src="https://imgur.com/jZO72TQ.png" width="500" height="auto" />

<p>
  Click "Generate", and voila!  You now have a customized meal plan to fit your needs.
  <br />
  You are able to regenerate meals you don't like, and pin your favorite meals to stay in rotation.
</p>
<img src="https://imgur.com/ekaufro.png" width=500 height="auto" />

<p>
  Clicking on a meal shows its nutrition information, ingredients, and cooking instructions.
  <br />
  The heart icon can be clicked to save a meal to your favorites (make an account first).
</p>
<img src="https://imgur.com/WmxK8rB.png" width=500 height="auto" />

<p>
  From your account, you are able to view the past meals you saved.
</p>
